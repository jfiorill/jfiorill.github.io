Crystal Clashers

This is a two player game with twelve different characters to choose from. In a similar to dodgeball style, players with launch crystals at each other until one players health is completely gone. However, every character has a unique ability and are granted buffs as their health gets lower. Additionally, each character has their own special move that charges up over time.
