Projects

Crystal Clashers

This is a two-player game with twelve different characters to choose from, created in Scratch. In a dodgeball-like style, players launch crystals at each other until one player's health is completely gone. Each character has a unique ability, gains buffs as their health decreases, and has a special move that charges over time.

Run Master

A mobile app created in Android Studio designed to track your runs. It has five different tabs: a pace calculator, workout suggestions for different race distances, an activity log, a news feed, and a toggle to switch between miles and kilometers.

NFL 2022-23 Season Simulator

A Python-based simulation of an NFL season that runs in Command Prompt. It processes each game, updates scores by quarter for anticipation, and progresses top teams into the playoffs. The simulation incorporates probabilities, giving teams with better records a slightly higher chance of winning.
