While I have learned these more in terms of doing some simple computer science based labs, I want to look to expand this more into being able to create games. During the summer of 2024 I spent a little bit of time learning C# through Unity, and in a future class I would like to learn how to use Unreal Engine, which uses C++. I think further enhancing my C++ skills will really help me in my college journey.
